Depth equirect example

The main canvas is a regular perspective viewport. The preview is rendered from a separate pano camera in the scene, converted to radial depth, then normalized with the measured min/max radial distance bounds, and log encoded before preview/export.

Click a primitive or the pano camera frustum to attach the gizmo. Use T for translate and R for rotate.
